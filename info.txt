KillKeys
http://killkeys.googlecode.com/
recover89@gmail.com

When enabled, keys specified in config.txt will be disabled.
By default, both windows keys and the menu key are disabled when in fullscreen.
Read config.txt for more info.

You can use -hide as a parameter to hide the tray icon.
